Authenticate screen

HBO

The above window pops up, with two call-to-action links that require users to either subscribe or log in.

 

Hulu

Users are promoted to start a free trial, or log into their account.

Netflix

Users are promoted to either sign in, or sign up for an account.

 

Amazon

Users are prompted to either sign in or create an Amazon account to start a free 30-day trial

YouTubeTV

Users cannot access content without signing in or signing up for YouTubeTV

Showtime

Users can browse content but when they click “Watch Now,” they are prompted to either sign-up for a free trial or sign into their account

Google Play

Users are prompted to sign in in order to watch the content. Clicking “Sign In” brings you to a sign-in page that allows you to create an account if you don’t have one.

 

FandangoNOW

Users are asked to join Fandango by creating a free account. If they already have an account, they can sign in to view content.

Play Screen

HBO

All controls becomes invisible 4 seconds after the video starts to play.

Hulu

All controls become invisible 4 seconds after the video starts to play.

Netflix

All controls become invisible 4 seconds after the video starts to play.

Amazon

All controls become invisible 4 seconds after the video starts to play. X-Ray feature and video scrubber appear when users hover or pause. X-Ray feature can be expanded on the top.

YouTubeTV

All controls become invisible 5 seconds after the video starts to play. Video controls, show and episode titles, TV rating, next episode button, and YouTubeTV toolbar at the bottom appear when users pause. No changes occur on hover.

Showtime

All controls become invisible 6 seconds after the video starts to play. Video controls, show and episode titles, season number, previous and next episode button, and exit button appear when users hover or pause.

Google Play

Video opens as an overlay over the series homepage. All controls become invisible 4 seconds after the video starts to play. Video scrubber, episode name, feedback icon, and controls appear when users hover or pause.

FandangoNOW

All controls become invisible 6 seconds after the video starts to play. The video scrubber and controls (including the return button) appear when users hover or pause.
